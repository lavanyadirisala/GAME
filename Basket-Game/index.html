INSTRUCTIONS

    • You have to collect objects falling down into a basket.

    • There are 4 dfferent types of objects.Each of them have a specific points value.

    • Each FRUIT : 7 points.Each EGG : 6 points.

    • Each FLOWER : 5 points.Each BOMB : -8 points.

    EASY

    • In this level objects will fall at a slower face.

    • And if the objects value missed by you is greater than 100 your game is over.

    MEDIUM

    • In this level objects will fall at a medium face.

    • And if the objects value missed by you is greater than 70 your game is over.

    DIFFICULT

    • In this level objects will fall at a fast face.

    • And if the objects value missed by you is greater than 50 your game is over.
